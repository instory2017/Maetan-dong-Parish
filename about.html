About the Parish

Maetan-dong Catholic Parish (Diocese of Suwon) was founded on January 29, 2002. Our patron saint is St. Thérèse of the Child Jesus. We welcome visitors and parishioners to join our liturgy, sacraments, and community life.

Address: 15, Ingye-ro 284beon-gil, Yeongtong-gu, Suwon, Gyeonggi-do
